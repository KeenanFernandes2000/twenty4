# twenty4

A mobile app for sharing your day with a private circle of friends — then letting it disappear.

## What it is

You capture photos and videos throughout the day. At the end of the day, twenty4 automatically turns them into a **30-second, beat-synced vertical montage** — no editing required. You review it, pick which private groups to share it with, and publish. Your friends react and comment. **Everything is permanently deleted 24 hours after you post it.**

It blends the authenticity of a daily recap, the ease of automatic video creation, and the freedom of content that doesn't stick around.

## The core loop

**Capture today → auto-generate a 30s recap → share to your groups → friends react → gone tomorrow.**

## What makes it different

- **Private by default** — content is shared only within friend groups you create or join, never publicly.
- **Effortless** — the montage is generated for you; there's no manual timeline editing.
- **Ephemeral** — published recaps and all their reactions and comments are hard-deleted after 24 hours. Nothing accumulates.
- **One recap per day** — a single, curated moment rather than an endless feed.

## Platform

Mobile-first, **Android first** (iOS to follow). A private friend-group experience — no public profiles, no follower counts, no ads.
